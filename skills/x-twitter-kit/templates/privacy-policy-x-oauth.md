# X/Twitter Connected Account Privacy Section

Connecting an X/Twitter account is optional. If you authorize access, the service may process account identifiers, profile information, posts, bookmarks, direct messages, lists, follows, likes, media, and other account data permitted by the scopes you approve.

The service uses connected-account data only to provide assistant functionality you request, such as reading bookmarked posts, summarizing posts or threads, searching account-accessible content, drafting posts or replies, managing lists, uploading media, or sending/reading direct messages if you separately approve direct-message access.

Connected-account data is not sold, used for advertising, or shared with third parties for their own marketing. It may be processed by service providers only as needed to operate the service, store credentials securely, troubleshoot, or perform actions you request.

You can revoke connected-account access at any time from your X/Twitter account settings. You may also contact the service operator to request deletion of retained connected-account data where applicable by law.
